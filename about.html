The Spice Garden

Sri Lanka, historically known as the Spice Island, is home to some of the world's most fragrant botanical treasures. Our perfumes start here, in the lush gardens of Matale and Kandy.

We harvest true Ceylon Cinnamon, Cardamom, and Clove at the peak of their potency, distilling their essence to create a warm, spicy heart that beats in every bottle of Ceylon Essence.
